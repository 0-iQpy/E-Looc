Where can I pay my community dues?

You can pay your community dues at the Barangay Treasurer's Office at the Barangay Hall during regular office hours. Bring your previous receipt for reference.

How do I report a community concern?

You can report community concerns through:

  • The "Report" button on our website
  • Visiting the Barangay Hall in person
  • Calling our hotline at 012-345-6789
  • Messaging our official Facebook page
